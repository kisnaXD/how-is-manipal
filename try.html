Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 05, 2025

1. Introduction

HowIsManipal Inc. operates HowIsManipal, a platform for reviewing aspects of the Manipal main campus. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your personal information.

2. Information Collection

We collect information you provide when submitting reviews, such as your name (optional) and review content. If you choose anonymity, no identifying information is linked to your review. We also collect usage data like IP addresses and browser details.

3. Use of Information

Your information is used to display reviews on HowIsManipal, improve our platform, and ensure compliance with our Terms and Conditions. Anonymous reviews are not tied to your identity.

4. Sharing Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless required by law or to protect the integrity of HowIsManipal. Anonymous reviews remain untraceable to individuals.

5. Content Guidelines

When submitting reviews, you must avoid vulgar, abusive, or offensive language. We may use your submitted content to monitor compliance but will not disclose your identity if you choose anonymity.

6. Security

We take steps to secure your data against unauthorized access, but no system is completely immune. Anonymous reviews are stored without personal identifiers.

7. Your Choices

You may request deletion of your reviews or personal data by contacting us. Note that anonymous reviews cannot be linked back to you for removal unless you provide proof of submission.

8. Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy as needed. Updates will be posted here, effective upon publication.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, reach out to: info@howismanipal.com.
